Haines, AK Water Utility

This map is intended as an example of what can be achieved through the use of OpenLayers and GeoServer techniques learned through UNM's GEOG 585L course. The map includes a basemap centering on Haines Borough, AK featuring 5 different assets of the public Water Utility. The assets include: Water Mains (Blue lines), Sewer Mains (Green lines), Fire Hydrants (Red circle with a cross), Man Hole Covers (Yellow circles), and Water Valves (Red circle with only the outlines visible).

Taken to completion, the map would ultimately include a popup window for each asset which could include pertinent data on the assets such as; condition, maintenance information, operators notes, and links to as-built diagrams and asset imagery.
