Haines, AK | Water Utility Map

Type of information dipicted:

water mains, sewer mains, manhole covers, and hydrants

Reason for selecting Haines, AK

I am in the process of creating a water utility map for the Haines Burough using ArcGIS and was interested to display this information using new technologies

Uses:

The purpose of this map will be to allow water operators a reference point for their water and waterwater lines, as well as some of their above ground assets.
