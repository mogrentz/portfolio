Haines, AK | Water Utility Map

Type of information dipicted: water mains, hydrants, hydrant legs, water valves, and pumping stations

Reason for selecting Haines, AK: I am in the process of creating a water utility map for the Haines Burough using ArcGIS and was interested to display this information using new technologies

Uses: The purpose of this map will be to allow water operators a reference point for their water mains as well as some of their above ground assets.
